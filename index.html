Friends of the Pearland Westside Library

Pearland Westside Library is one of the most active in the Brazoria County Library system. Join us!

An annual membership is only $20.

Membership dues provide funding for unique programs, extra materials, library events, education, and enhanced services.


A new standalone library building is already underway, bringing more space and new services for library patrons!

How to Become a Member

An annual membership is $20.

You may pay dues in person at the library desk or pay/contribute through the online options available to the right.

What We Do

Your membership will put you on our mailing list for updates, meetings, and volunteer opportunities. Friends of the Pearland Westside Library members may donate time, talent, or simply watch their dollars at work.


Many library services, including Friends meetings are currently conducted virtually.

Contact Us

Have questions? Email us at contact@pearlandwestsidelibraryfriends.org

Your financial contributions matter.
You may pay your membership dues or donate contributions through these easy and secure methods.

 

The Friends of Pearland Westside Library is a non-profit 501C3 Tax Exempt group
committed to improving learning through volunteer efforts and member support of the Pearland Westside Library.
